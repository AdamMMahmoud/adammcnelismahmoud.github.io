I evaluated these models across seven linkage criteria (focusing on single linkage), using dendrograms, silhouette scores, Calinskiâ€“Harabasz indices, and Daviesâ€“Bouldin indices to quantify results. Ultimately, I applied these clustered season definitions to pricing regressions, resulting in reduced multicollinearity compared to standard 13-period models and improved demand predictions.
